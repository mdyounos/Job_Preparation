## üìà Excel Formulas Used

  `=SUM(B2:B8)`

- **Average Demand**:  
  `=AVERAGE(B2:B8)`

- **Maximum Demand**:  
  `=MAX(B2:B8)`

- **Minimum Demand**:  
  `=MIN(B2:B8)`

- **Demand Fluctuation (Max - Min)**:  
  `=MAX(B2:B8) - MIN(B2:B8)`
